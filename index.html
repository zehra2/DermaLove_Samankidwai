Skin Treatments & Semi permanent Makeup.

We are dealing with two types of Microneedling Procedures PhiBright (Skin Treatment) and PhiContour (Semi permanent makeup) developed by PHIACADEMY

Microneedling
  • Microneedling is the process which will improve your skin by sitmulating micro circulation and the natural processes of collagen production and regeneration to give you healthy and ulta glow skin.
  • PhiBright is an upgrade of the microneedling technique that enables personalized treatments adapted to individual needs of different skin types and conditions.
  • Microneedling Treatment Provides smoother, firmer and toned skin.
  • The treatment uses a device to deliver the energy in the form of plasma to rejuvenate skin by improving facial lines and wrinkles, skin pigmentation associated with photo-ageing and other skin irregularities.
Advantages of Microneedling
  • Treatment results include: skin hydration, increased skin tone and smoothness causing existing wrinkles to be less visible and skin younger looking. Results become visible 24 hours after the procedure and shall last for a few months. The procedure is repeated up to three times ten to fourteen days apart.
  • The process triggers an instant and natural contraction and tightening of the skin’s fibres to create a lifting, remodelling and rejuvenating effect. The end result is like a face lift without any of the painful surgery or recovery involved.

PHICONTOUR

PhiContour is a skill of semi-permanent drawing of eyelids or lips.

PhiContour offers different techniques to correct natural defects but also to highlight contours.

With PhiContour lip and eyeliner pigmentation, the perfect shape is measured based on the golden ratio (1.618 Phi) and facial morphology. The PhiApp is used to calculate the symmetry or suitable shape of the lips and eyebrows. We offer various techniques to correct flaws or highlight contours. Attention: A PhiContour treatment cannot be done if you have eczema, seborrheic dermatitis, acne, zits or any other skin alteration in the pigmentation area etc. Should you have further questions, please contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any query you can ask us .

  • What Skin problems are Treatable by Microneedling?

    Micro needling is proven to improve the following conditions: mild to moderate acne scars, fine lines, deep wrinkles, sagging skin, rough skin texture, pore size, sun damage, and stretch marks.

  • What Does the Treatment Consist Of?

    The treatment includes cleaning and numbing the skin. A derma stamp pen or dermaroller with microneedles is used to produce thousands of micro punctures in the skin. Vitamin-C serum or Hyaluronic acid are applied after the treatment to enhance skin renewal and collagen growth.

  • Does Skin Needling Work on Other Skin Color?

    Microneedling is safe for all skin types and colors. It doesn’t produce post inflammatory hyperpigmentation.

  • How Many Treatments Do I Need to See Results?

    The number of treatments needed depends on the severity of your skin condition. Many people reported seeing noticeable improvements after 1 to 4 treatments.

  • Are There Post Infections?

    Micro punctures or micro wounds close up quickly within 1 hour. Therefore infections are very rare.
